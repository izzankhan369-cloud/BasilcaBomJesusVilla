The Villa

Where Colonial
Grace Meets
Tropical Serenity

Nestled amid centuries-old spice groves and Portuguese-era architecture, Casa Verde Villa is a private sanctuary for those who seek beauty in slowness. Originally built in 1897, the estate has been lovingly restored to preserve every carved teak doorway, terracotta floor tile, and frangipani-scented courtyard.

Eight curated suites, a private plunge pool, and a dedicated butler ensure that each stay is as effortless as it is unforgettable. This is Old Goa at its most intimate.
